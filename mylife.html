MyLife in CEG

I am Pavithra J currently pursuing Msc Computer Science(5 years Integrated) in Department of Mathematics. My life in CEG was fantastic.I lived in my dream college.I enjoyed a lot with my friends and enjoyed each and every second in my college. This college thought lot of good things through both good and bad exprience. Lets see deep about my exprience in my college.

First Year

First I got admission in CEG through counselling,that was the first I visited CEG.Then my first day of class started on August 21,2017.It was one of the memorable day where I found lot of new faces.I interacted with few people in my class. Later,the days are passed by exploring things in campus.The assesment are geared up and we all started studying.Intially it was tough to study. Then finally culturals are began,we enjoyed each and every programme.First year was very slow and itwas a hardship because we are all missed our families.Finally vaccation had came we all happy to pack our things and vaccated the hostels.

Second Year

Friends became as family and we enjoyed a lot in the second year.We exprienced the college and went to each corners of college and do fun.In that stage studies were not more important to us.In the hostel we make fun,they were many sleepless nights while we dance,sing,gossips...

Even though there are lot of places to visit in chennai we didn't went out of campus because of fear,we went all over our campus and had fun with other department friends.

Third Year

We went to marnia beach,besant nagar beach,and more places in chennai.We participated in all the events conducted by our college. We got attached to our staffes.We got familiar with the mess staffs,hostel and canteen staffes. We went to Industrial visit to Neyveli,it was our first industrial visit.We throughly enjoyed the IV especillaly the bus dances.

We organized several events in both department and in our college.I had been a part as HR team of techofes.In our Department we organised CEG Speaks where ech and every person can show case thier own interest they can explain the things that they see in their prespective.

Fourth Year

First half went well,We were very excited about our internship.we very preparing for our interviews.Though, there is a tough schedule we went for Another Industrial visit to ISRO,it was out of statte we very very eager about the travel,we explored lot of things and learnt lot of things through this IV.And Symposium happened in our department that will happen early once (MATHIX).We enjoyed and took so many pictures on that day because of traditional wears.

After all the fun,there was sudden lockdown,due to COVID-19.We all shocked and we afraid of our semesters,there was big happy news that all of our college student got all pass.Then I got my Internship in Kloudone,where lot of technical and behaviourial stuffs

Final Year

The odd semester went with the online classes,then we all awaited for our college reopen finally the colleges are opened on September after a long gap,we all excited to meet our friends and college.Then the normal routine starts up with offline classes and examination We thought it was the final sem to enjoy so we used to play games(MAFIA) even at nights.The things went smoothly,but sadly again Government announced Lockdown.
